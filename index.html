How are winners chosen?

Every entrant becomes a car with equal odds. The entire race — every overtake, mistake and photo finish — is generated from the seed shown in the controls, not from live randomness or your device speed. Re-running with the same list and seed always produces the identical result, so anyone can verify the draw wasn't rigged. Change the seed (🎲) for a fresh independent draw.
